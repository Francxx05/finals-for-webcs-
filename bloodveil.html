About the Story

A story of corruption, dictatorship, suffering, and betrayal, Bloodveil is the dark counterpart to Bloom Into You. While familiar faces appear as side characters, the focus shifts to the corrupt Aros corporation and its ruthless CEO.

Aros's one goal is to revolutionize the world by capturing the power of Daniel, an otherworldly being, to unlock humanity's latent potential.

In a world filled with antagonists and anti-heroes, every character walks a line between right and wrong, forcing players to question where true evil lies.

Characters

Robin

The Tattoo Artist with a Hidden Lab

Adopted by the CEO of the corrupt Aros corporation, Robin enjoyed a privileged education. However, she was abandoned by her parents, who were horrified by her entity's ability to make people explode with its reflection. She now operates from the shadows, her past a closely guarded secret.

Alexander (Alex)

The Betrayed Scientist

A brilliant former Aros scientist, Alex experimented to replicate the otherworldly power of Daniel, hoping to revolutionize the world. However, he committed treason after a bitter betrayal by the CEO, and now works to undermine the company he once helped build.

Daniel

The Otherworldly Invader

An antagonist from another dimension where his kind has blue blood and can transform into monstrous beings. Raised by a dictator, Daniel seeks to invade Earth and eradicate humanity, viewing them as a useless and inferior species.

Gameplay Preview

Bloodveil is being developed in Unity, chosen for its flexibility in crafting both narrative-driven experiences and complex gameplay systems.

Inspired by the branching narratives and impactful choices of 'Detroit: Become Human', every decision you make in Bloodveil will have lasting consequences, shaping the story and the fate of its characters.

The gameplay will draw inspiration from the survival horror and resource management mechanics of the 'Resident Evil' series, blending intense action with strategic thinking.

About the Creator

Hello! I’m Francheska Rivera, also known online as “Juno.”

That dream eventually became Bloom Into You and its dark counterpart, Bloodveil.

The concept for Bloodveil first began when I was 13, born from daydreams and unrefined character ideas. It was a fantasy that stayed with me, and now it finally has a name. Bloodveil is currently a work-in-progress game portfolio, and I'm still developing other key characters—like the CEO of Aros, Robin's right-hand man, and Alexander's lover—who will be crucial to the unfolding events of the story.
